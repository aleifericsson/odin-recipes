Ingredients

  • rice grains
  • Indonesian kecap (~ 3tbsp)
  • egg
  • Indonesian kerupul (1 pc, not to be confused with the ONE PIECE)
  • salt and pepper
  • green onions/scallions whatever you wanna call them
  • butter
  • onions and garlic

Methodology

  1. steam the rice until its edible
  2. cut up the garlic, green onions and onion
  3. sautee all of them with butter but maybe put the onions in first to fry the up longer
  4. take it all out and cook the egg however you want on the pan (but if its not sunny side up its a crime)
  5. in a bowl mix the rice with some kecap (season to taste) along with salt and pepper and all the veggies
  6. pat the rice into the bowl shape and plate it with the egg and kerupuk (add cucumber slices and onions if you want the illusion that it's healthy)
